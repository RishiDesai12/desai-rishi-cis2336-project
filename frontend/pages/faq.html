Frequently Asked Questions

Click on any question below to see the answer.

How much does it cost to use ArtConnect?

ArtConnect is completely free for both artists looking to showcase their portfolios and visitors wanting to discover local art.

How do I submit my artwork to the gallery?

You can submit your art by navigating to the "Submit Artwork" page and filling out the required form with your details, artwork category, and description.

Can I purchase artwork directly through this website?

ArtConnect serves as a directory rather than a storefront. If a piece is listed with a price, you can reach out to the artist directly at one of our local events to arrange a purchase.

Are the local art events free to attend?

Many of the events listed on our platform are free to the public, though certain specialized workshops may require a small materials fee. Please check the specific event details on our Events page.

How often is the gallery updated?

We review and approve new artist submissions on a rolling basis, so the gallery is typically updated several times a week with fresh local talent.
